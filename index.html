About Us

Preserving Knowledge, Inspiring Minds Since 1951

Our Legacy

Delhi Public Library, established in 1951 as a pilot project under the UNESCO Public Library Manifesto, stands as one of the oldest and most prestigious public libraries in India. We are committed to democratizing access to knowledge and fostering a culture of reading and learning.

Our Vision

To be the premier public library system in India, providing universal access to information, supporting lifelong learning, and serving as a cultural hub that connects communities through the power of knowledge.
