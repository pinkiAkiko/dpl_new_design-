About Us

Empowering communities through knowledge and learning

Our Mission

The Department of Public Libraries is committed to providing accessible, high-quality library services to all citizens of India. We aim to promote literacy, support education, and foster a culture of reading and lifelong learning.

Our Vision

To create a comprehensive network of public libraries that serve as community hubs for knowledge, learning, and cultural enrichment, ensuring that every citizen has access to information and educational resources.
